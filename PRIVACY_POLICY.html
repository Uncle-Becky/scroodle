Scroodle Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-12

This Privacy Policy explains what information Scroodle ("the App") processes and how it is used.

1. Data Processed

To operate gameplay, the App processes limited Reddit-provided context and gameplay events, such as:

  • Reddit user ID and username (for room membership and scoreboard display)
  • Post/subreddit context needed for installation-scoped matchmaking
  • Gameplay inputs (drawing points, guesses, system events)
  • Operational metadata (timestamps, room and queue state)

2. How Data Is Used

  • Match players into rooms and run rounds
  • Synchronize realtime gameplay and scores
  • Maintain service reliability and prevent abuse

3. Storage and Retention

  • Gameplay state is stored in Reddit Devvit Redis for active sessions.
  • Rooms and queue data are periodically cleaned up by scheduled tasks.
  • No payment data is collected by this App.

4. Sharing

The App does not sell personal data. Data is only processed as needed to provide gameplay through Reddit's Developer Platform.

5. Security

Reasonable safeguards are used within platform capabilities, including server-authoritative state handling and scoped settings/secrets.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may fetch externally hosted word lists if configured by moderators (wordlistUrl setting). Usage of such sources is controlled by installation settings.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under applicable minimum age requirements for Reddit platform usage.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy questions related to this App, contact the app maintainer via the associated Reddit app listing.
